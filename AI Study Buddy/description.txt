AI Study Buddy (Rule based chat assistant in python)

Objective -> 
to create a conversational ai assistant using python's core logic.

What to do ->
In this project, we will build a mini AI chatbot that can interact with users. 
The chatbot will understand simple messages like "hello", :motivate me", or "python"
and respond with friendly, intelligent answers.

This is a rule based chatbot, it doesn't use real AI analysis yet, but it behaves intelligently with python logics.

# Sample output

🤖 Hello! I'm shub's study buddy!
You can talk to me. Type "bye" anytime to exit.

You: hello
Bot: hi

You: Who are You
Bot: I'm your friendly AI study buddy created by shubha,

You: motivate me
Bot: Keep going! Every bug you fix makes you a better coder

You: bye
Bot: Goodbye, Nice to talk to you.

# features
- respond to simple users inputs
- works continously untill typed bye
- easy to customize with new keywords
